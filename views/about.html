About Me

I have been a professional developer for a very long time and gained a lot of experience along the way. This experience comes with a particular set of skills that have led me to where I am today. My expertise has been primarily in front end development, but I have had experience designing websites and light backend work including PHP and familiarity with a .NET environment, including content management system's like Wordpress and Umbraco.

It is not enough to be well versed in different languages and technologies, but also other skills are required. With these skills comes the ability to solve problems, the need for attention to detail, and finding ways to improve efficiencies. I am rarely satisfied with knowing "just enough" to get by. I crave to learn and gain more knowledge so I can provide more value with every website or web application I create.
